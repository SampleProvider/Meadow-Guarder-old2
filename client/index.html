RULES:

Do not make accounts with inappropriate names. Do not make accounts that inpersonate another player. Do not advertize using your username or in the chat. No random messages and spamming the chat. No in-game items and accounts may be traded for REAL WORLD MONEY.

Violation of any of these rules may result in your account(s) being suspended and/or deleted.

HOW TO GET ADMIN:

1. Don't beg for admin.
2. Play Meadow Guarder for over 1 month.
3. Complete the contribution form.
4. Contribute by submiting at least 5 helpful bugs or suggestions that the devs would not have thought of otherwise. If it is a suggestion, it must have been added into the game.

You should not be trying to get admin so you can show it off. You should be trying to get admin so you can stop hackers/cheaters.

WHEN YOU ARE ADMIN:

1. Don't break any of the rules.
2. Don't abuse your powers.
3. If you don't contribute much, your admin powers will be revoked.

ADMIN LEVELS:

There are 3 admin levels: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3.
When you first get admin, you start off at Level 1. This allows you to kill and kick people.
Requirements for Level 2: 50 issues/suggestions, trustworthy, joined discord server.
At Level 2, you can teleport, ban players, and rickroll players.
There is no way to obtain Level 3, it is just for sp.

Please help support Meadow Guarder by Watching and Starring our Github page.

HOW TO PLAY: Use WASD or Arrow keys to move. Left click to use items and right click to interact.

If you see a glitch, check our Github page for if this glitch is already posted. If you don't see a already posted glitch, feel free to post a glitch! Our Github page is here.
